About Us

"The Mahindra Thar has 2 Diesel Engine and 1 Petrol Engine on offer. The Diesel engine is 2184 cc and 1497 cc while the Petrol engine is 1997 cc . It is available with Automatic & Manual transmission.Depending upon the variant and fuel type the Thar has a mileage of 15.2 kmpl & Ground clearance of Thar is 226mm. The Thar is a 4 seater 4 cylinder car and has length of 3985mm, width of 1855mm and a wheelbase of 2450mm."

Features & Technology

More Power

The Mahindra Thar features a choice of potent petrol and diesel engines, delivering robust torque and horsepower for versatile on-road and off-road performance. These powertrains ensure a thrilling driving experience and underscore the Thar's capability as a dependable and rugged SUV, ideal for adventure seekers and enthusiasts.

Advanced Technology

The Mahindra Thar prioritizes safety with features like anti-lock braking system (ABS), airbags, a robust chassis, and electronic stability control. These elements work together to ensure a secure driving experience on and off-road, making the Thar a safe choice for adventurous journeys.

High Performance

The Mahindra Thar incorporates advanced technology, including touchscreen infotainment systems, smartphone integration, and electronic stability control. These features enhance convenience, connectivity, and safety, making the Thar a modern and tech-savvy SUV for both urban and off-road adventures.
